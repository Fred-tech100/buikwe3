Who We Are

A community-rooted foundation working for every girl's future

Buikwe Foundation is a grassroots non-profit based in Buikwe District, Uganda. We exist to empower girls and young women through education, skills training, and community support — helping them become self-reliant, confident individuals who transform their communities.

Our Mission

To empower girls and young women in Buikwe through education, skills training, and community support.

Our Vision

A society where every girl can complete her education and participate fully in socioeconomic development.

Our Values
What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Empowerment

We equip girls with skills, knowledge and confidence to take charge of their own futures.

Integrity

We act with honesty and transparency in every relationship and every donation entrusted to us.

Sustainability

We build programs and local capacity that keep creating impact long after we've moved on.

Community

We work with families, elders and local leaders, because lasting change is built together.

One training centre. Hundreds of futures rewritten.

Since opening our skills centre in Buikwe Town, girls who once saw no path beyond early marriage now run tailoring shops, salons and small businesses of their own — and are mentoring the next class behind them.
